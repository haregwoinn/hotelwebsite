HAREGEWOIN HOTEL

Welcome to Haregewoin hotel which is located in Kombolcha where most industrial activities of the country take place. Our hotel is only 15 minutes away from the Kombolcha Airport. We currently have 70 spacious rooms with a beautiful panoramic views of Kombolcha. Our multilingual and well trained staff is ready to serve you and cater to your needs. We also offer free Airport / Hotel shuttle service. Whether you are a business traveler or on vacation, you will definitely enjoy our well furnished rooms and comfortable atmosphere in our Hotel. You will have access to free wi-fi internet connection through out the Hotel.Our Bar and Restaurant will be your choice to dine and enjoy drinks. Room service is available 24 hours a day 7 days a week.
